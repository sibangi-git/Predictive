Why we chose this model:

We chose this model because it had the best R-squared and adjusted R-squared values compared to other models. The residuals were also close to white noise, due to the Ljung Box test offering the null hypothesis of the residuals being white noise, the P-value is greater than .05; meaning we fail to reject the null hypothesis. By adding lagged log values (lag_log_total), we significantly reduced autocorrelation at lag 1, leading to a more accurate model. The R-squared (~70%) confirms that the model explains most of the variance. Using lagged variables improves predictions while keeping other variables meaningful, making the model both reliable and easy to interpret. This method is simpler than ARIMA but still effectively captures the relationship between past and current passenger counts.
